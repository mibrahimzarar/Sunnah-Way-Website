Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why do I need to allow location access?

A: Location access is required to provide accurate prayer times based on your current location. This ensures you get the most precise prayer times for your area.

Q: How often are prayer times updated?

A: Prayer times are calculated based on your location and the selected calculation method. They update automatically when you change location or calculation method.

Q: Can I use the app offline?

A: Yes! Prayer times are cached locally, and the Qibla compass works offline. Daily content is also available without internet connection.

Q: How do I change the prayer time calculation method?

A: Go to Settings in the app to change the calculation method. We support multiple methods including Muslim World League, Islamic Society of North America, and others.

Q: How do I track my sunnah practices?

A: Navigate to the Sunnah tab to view available practices and challenges. Tap on any practice to mark it as completed for the day.

Troubleshooting

Prayer Times Not Loading

  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure location services are enabled
  • Try refreshing the app

Qibla Compass Not Working

  • Calibrate your device by moving it in a figure-8 pattern
  • Ensure you're not near magnetic interference
  • Check that location services are enabled

Notifications Not Working

  • Check app notification permissions in device settings
  • Ensure the app is not in battery optimization mode
  • Verify notification settings within the app
