Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Information We Collect

Sunnah Way is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Location Data: We request location access to provide accurate prayer times. This data is processed locally and not stored on our servers.
  • Authentication: When you sign in with Google, we receive basic profile information (name, email) to personalize your experience.
  • App Usage: We collect anonymous analytics to improve app performance and user experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide accurate prayer times based on your location
  • Personalize your app experience
  • Send prayer time notifications and daily reminders
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

Data Storage and Security

  • Most data is stored locally on your device
  • User preferences and settings are synced securely via Supabase
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission
  • We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information

Third-Party Services

  • Google Authentication: For secure sign-in functionality
  • Supabase: For backend services and data storage
  • Aladhan API: For prayer time calculations

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics collection
  • Disable location services
